## How pricing works

OEC engagements follow a two-step model:

```
Step 1 → Maturity Assessment   (hourly · billed separately)
Step 2 → Project Mandate       (fixed project price · based on assessment score)
```

The Assessment is always the entry point. It determines which project mandate applies  
and which roles are required. Assessment fees are **not** deducted from the project price.

**There are no retainers.**  
Every mandate has a defined scope, clear deliverables, and an end.  
When a project is complete, the next company has the opportunity to work with the collective.  
If a client needs further support, they request a new mandate.

## Step 1 – Partner Ecosystem Maturity Assessment

**Role:** Level 2 – Assessment Lead  
**Rate:** CHF 175 / hour  
**Duration:** 1–4 hours depending on company size and complexity

| Company Size | Complexity | Duration | Total |
|---|---|---|---|
| 5–8 employees | No existing partnerships | 1h | CHF 175 |
| 8–12 employees | First partnerships in place | 2h | CHF 350 |
| 12–20 employees | Multiple active partners | 3h | CHF 525 |
| Multi-cloud · active MDF | High complexity | 4h | CHF 700 |

**What the assessment delivers:**

- Full 75-point evaluation (5 dimensions × 3 questions × 5 points)
- Structured interview with relevant stakeholders
- Score report with maturity band classification
- Gap analysis: what is missing to reach the next level?
- Prioritised recommendations
- Project mandate recommendation (tier and roles)

> **Note:** The assessment fee is billed separately and is not credited toward a subsequent project mandate.

## Revenue Split

All mandates run through the OEC collective.  
Every invoice is issued by VRC. Payout to the network member happens after client payment is received.

```
70%  →  Network Member (executing the mandate)
20%  →  VRC (invoicing, contract management, payment risk)
10%  →  Lua Chea (mentoring, community building, methodology infrastructure)
```

**Why VRC takes 20%:**  
VRC issues the invoice, manages the contract, and carries the payment risk.  
The network member does not chase payments or handle administration.  
Payout is guaranteed once the client has paid — not before.

**Why Lua Chea takes 10%:**  
Lua Chea provides mentoring, community building, and maintains the open-source  
methodology infrastructure that makes OEC mandates possible.

## Pricing Principles

**Project prices, not retainers.**  
Every mandate has a defined scope, clear deliverables, and an end.  
This protects both sides: the client knows exactly what they are paying for,  
and the collective can serve the next company when the work is done.

**Assessment first.**  
No project mandate is scoped without an assessment. This protects the client  
from paying for the wrong level of engagement.

**Transparent roles.**  
Every project mandate states which OEC roles are involved.  
The client knows what expertise they are getting and at what level.

**No hidden markup.**  
The project price is what it is. The revenue split is public.  
70% goes to the person doing the work.
