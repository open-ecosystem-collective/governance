## How to read this log

**Version numbers follow Semantic Versioning:**
- `v1.0` → Initial release
- `v1.1` → Minor clarification or improvement (no structural change)
- `v1.2` → Additional content within existing structure
- `v2.0` → Significant structural change or new core element

**Each entry records:**
- What changed and in which document
- Who proposed it
- How the decision was made (Lazy Consensus / Supermajority / Founder decision)
- Date merged

**Personal details of members are never recorded in this log.**  
Membership decisions are noted as decisions of the collective,
not as records of individuals.
